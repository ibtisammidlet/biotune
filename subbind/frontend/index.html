Ki nM = mean how well the substance can bind to a receptor =the lower the better

IC50 = Half-maximal inhibitory concentration = the lower the better = mean antagonist

EC50 = Half maximal effective concentration = the lower the better = mean agonist

Kd nM = The equilibrium dissociation constant. represents the concentration of radioligand. occupying half of the maximum receptor. = the lower the better = its mean the how well the drug can bind to receptor (like ki nm)

Emax/ imax = efficacy = the higher the better = its not available in bindingdb.org
